I am Lokesh Veeramacheneni, PhD student in Computer Vision Group headed by [Prof. Dr. Juergen Gall](https://pages.iai.uni-bonn.de/gall_juergen/).
I am interested in vision problems, because I believe vision is one of the important basic sensory system to navigate this complex world. My research interets are Deep Neural Networks, Tensor mathematics, and Uncertainty quantification.
Along with [Dr. Moritz Wolter](https://www.wolter.tech/?page_id=455), I teach courses such as Foundations of Machine Learning and Advanced Machine Learning at High Performance Computing and Analytics (HPCA) Lab. My PhD is being funded by HPCA part of [WestAI](https://westai.de/) research grant.

Prior to my PhD, I received by M.Sc degree in Autonomous Systems from Hochschule Bonn-Rhein-Sieg in 2022 and an Bachelor degree in Electronics and Communications Engineering from KL University. In addition, I have a 2 years of professional experience as software developer in India, and Germany.
