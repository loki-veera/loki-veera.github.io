Bonn, Germany

I am a PhD student at High Performance Computing/Analytics Lab at University of Bonn, advised by Prof.Juergen Gall. My area of research is deep learning for Computer Vision. In particular, I enjoy working with Diffusion and Video models. Prior to my PhD, I received my M.Sc degree in Autonomous Systems at Hochschule Bonn-Rhein-Sieg, Germany. Later worked as software developer at RE: Gmbh, Köln. In addition to research, I also teach few modules in the courses offered by HPC/A Lab.
